What is Dream Convention

Where
Dreams
Come to Life

Dream Convention is a one-night leadership event that brings together the brightest entrepreneurial minds from the University of St. Thomas and the University of Minnesota.

Hear directly from four leaders who built something from nothing. Their stories, their failures, their breakthroughs. Then take the stage and pitch your own.

The speakers are your judges. Real prize money. A real shot at turning your idea into something more.
